OVERVIEW:
Lotus Sutra Goa is a boutique beach property in the laid-back village of Arambol in North Goa. Set in a peaceful environment, the property combines cottages and premium building rooms surrounded by lush greenery. Total 57 units — 20 building rooms and 37 cottages. The property is ideal for couples, families, and small groups seeking a relaxed Goa experience with easy access to Arambol Beach.
