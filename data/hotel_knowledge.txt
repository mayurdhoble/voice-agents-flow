OVERVIEW:
The Grand Orchid Hotel is a 5-star luxury hotel located in the heart of Koregaon Park, Pune's most vibrant and upscale neighborhood. We offer world-class hospitality, modern amenities, and a serene atmosphere perfect for both business and leisure travelers. The hotel has 120 well-appointed rooms and suites with stunning views of the Pune cityscape and the lush greenery of Koregaon Park.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

Q: Do you have airport pickup?
A: Yes, we offer airport transfers. Sedan is INR 1,500 and SUV is INR 2,500 one way. Please inform us your flight details 24 hours in advance.

Q: Is breakfast included?
A: Yes, complimentary buffet breakfast at The Terrace restaurant is included with all room bookings.

Q: Do you allow pets?
A: Yes, we are pet-friendly. There is an additional charge of INR 500 per night. Please inform us in advance.

Q: Is the hotel near Koregaon Park restaurants and cafes?
A: Yes, the hotel is walking distance from German Bakery, Dario's, and many popular Koregaon Park restaurants.

Q: Do you have a swimming pool?
A: Yes, we have a heated outdoor pool open from 6 AM to 10 PM, plus a separate kids pool.

Q: What is the check-in age requirement?
A: Guests must be 18 years or older to check in. Minors must be accompanied by adults.

Q: Do you offer special rates for long stays?
A: Yes, stays of 7 nights or more get 15% discount. Stays of 30+ nights get 25% discount.

Q: Is there a gym?
A: Yes, our fitness center is open 24 hours and is complimentary for all guests.

Q: Can I host a wedding at the hotel?
A: Absolutely. We have the Grand Ballroom (500 guests) and outdoor Garden Lawn (300 guests). Our events team will be happy to assist with planning.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash in INR.

Q: Is there parking?
A: Yes, complimentary valet parking is available for all guests.

Q: Do you have vegetarian and vegan options?
A: Yes, our restaurants offer extensive vegetarian and vegan menus. Please inform us of any dietary requirements.
